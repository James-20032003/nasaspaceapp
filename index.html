Welcome to The Earth System

This project explores the interconnections between Earth’s systems, and demonstrate how changes in one system can alter another system.e following focus areas interact under the influence of climate change: air quality, biodiversity, natural disasters, agriculture, water resources, sea levels, renewable energy, wildfires, and greenhouse gasses. Changes in Earth’s climate can ripple through several of these areas
