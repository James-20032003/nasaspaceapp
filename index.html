Welcome to the Ecosystem Education Project

This project is dedicated to educating individuals about the importance of ecosystems and biodiversity. Our aim is to raise awareness and foster a deeper understanding of the environmental issues we face today. Join us in our journey to protect our planet and its precious resources.

Gallery of Ecosystems Affected by Climate Change

Amazon Rainforest

The Amazon rainforest is one of the most biodiverse places on Earth and is severely affected by deforestation and climate change.

Coral Reefs

Coral reefs are dying at an alarming rate due to rising ocean temperatures and acidification, which pose a threat to marine life.
